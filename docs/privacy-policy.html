Privacy Policy

App: Nomad Translator
Developer: Minh Kien Ngo
Last updated: May 6, 2025

1. No Data Collection

Nomad Translator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All translation is performed entirely on-device. No text, voice input, or photos you use in the app are sent to any server.

2. Camera & Microphone Access

The app may request access to your camera and microphone solely to enable photo-based text extraction and voice input for translation. This data is processed locally on your device and never leaves it.

3. Photo Library Access

The app may request access to your photo library to allow you to select images for text extraction. Images are processed on-device only and are not stored or shared.

4. No Third-Party Analytics or Advertising

We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking tools.

5. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
