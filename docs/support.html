Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work without internet?

Yes. Once you download the language pack in Settings, all translations work fully offline — no internet connection required.

How do I download the language pack?

Open the app → tap the Settings icon (top right) → tap Download. The pack is about 1.1 GB and only needs to be downloaded once.

The translation seems slow — is that normal?

Translation is done entirely on your device using an AI model, which takes 2–5 seconds depending on your iPhone model. Newer devices are faster.

Can I translate photos or handwriting?

Yes. Tap the camera icon in the input area to take a photo or choose one from your library. The app will extract the text and translate it.

Which languages are supported?

The app supports 33+ languages including English, Vietnamese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Thai, French, Spanish, German, Arabic, Hindi, and more.

How do I delete the language pack?

Go to Settings → scroll down → tap Delete Pack. This frees up storage space but you'll need to re-download to use translations again.
